About

CreatED is a 24-hour hardware hackathon organised by the University of Edinburgh Embedded & Robotics Society. The hackathon will see students from some of the best universities across the UK, come together to build an innovative hardware hack. We are the only student-run hackathon in the UK with a focus on hardware! Hackers will be producing physical devices, not just software projects.
